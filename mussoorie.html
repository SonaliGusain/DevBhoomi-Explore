Mussoorie, known as the 'Queen of Hills,' is one of India's most popular hill stations, offering stunning landscapes, colonial architecture, and adventure activities.

Most Popular Places in Mussoorie

Lal Tibba - The highest point in Mussoorie offering panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Kempty Falls - A stunning waterfall perfect for picnics and swimming.

Gun Hill - A famous viewpoint accessible by ropeway, offering breathtaking views.

The Mall Road - A lively street with shops, restaurants, and colonial charm.

Company Garden - A beautiful garden with colorful flowers and an artificial lake.

Mussoorie Lake - A peaceful lake where you can enjoy boating and nature views.

Landour - A quiet retreat with British-era charm and stunning scenery.

Jharipani Falls - A hidden waterfall, ideal for trekking and relaxation.

George Everest's House - The historic home of Sir George Everest, offering mesmerizing views.

Bhadraj Temple - A scenic temple located on a hill, known for its religious significance and trekking trails.
